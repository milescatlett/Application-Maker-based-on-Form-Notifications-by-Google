Application Maker was created from a script that expands the capability of a typical Google Form to make it more like a fully-fledged application. This includes the ability to create a document from a template and email that link, along with a completion message, to the person who completed the form. In addition, emails can be sent to other recipients and the application includes other functions and customizations.

At a minimum, you should write a title for your new document and place values in your document template on the Template Headers page, and choose a document template and target folder using the Template Picker and Folder Picker.

Application Maker is based on and is an expanded form of the Google Form Notifications sample add-on. While it's an expanded form of the original, it has similar limitations.

The number of notifications this add-on produces are limited by the owner's available email quota; it will not send email notifications if the owner's daily email quota has been exceeded. Collaborators using this add-on on the same form will be able to adjust the notification settings, but will not be able to disable the notification triggers set by other collaborators.
